Employee Benefits Package

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. HEALTH INSURANCE
- Health insurance coverage begins on the first day of employment
- Company covers 80% of premium costs for employee and dependents
- Three plan options available: HMO, PPO, and High-Deductible Health Plan
- Prescription drug coverage included in all plans
- Dental and vision insurance available at employee cost
- Open enrollment period: November 1-30 annually

2. RETIREMENT BENEFITS
- 401(k) plan with company matching up to 6% of salary
- Matching contributions begin after 90 days of employment
- Vesting schedule: 25% after 1 year, 50% after 2 years, 100% after 3 years
- Investment options include target-date funds and index funds
- Annual contribution limit: $23,000 (2024 IRS limit)

3. PAID TIME OFF
- Paid vacation accrues at 1.25 days per month (15 days annually)
- Sick leave: 8 days per year
- Personal days: 3 days per year
- Floating holidays: 2 days per year
- Vacation time increases with tenure (20 days after 5 years, 25 days after 10 years)

4. FAMILY AND MEDICAL LEAVE
- Eligible for FMLA after 12 months of employment
- Up to 12 weeks of unpaid leave per year
- Job protection during leave period
- Health insurance benefits continue during leave
- Short-term disability insurance available after 90 days

5. PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
- Annual training budget of $2,000 per employee
- Conference attendance and certification fees covered
- Tuition reimbursement up to $5,000 per year
- Internal training programs and workshops
- Mentorship program available for career development

6. WORK-LIFE BALANCE
- Flexible work hours (core hours 10 AM - 3 PM)
- Remote work options (see Remote Work Policy)
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP) for counseling and support
- On-site fitness center with personal training sessions
- Wellness program with health screenings and incentives

7. ADDITIONAL BENEFITS
- Life insurance coverage equal to 2x annual salary
- Long-term disability insurance at 60% of salary
- Commuter benefits program for public transportation
- Employee discount program with local businesses
- Annual company retreat and team building events

8. BENEFITS ENROLLMENT
- New employee enrollment within 30 days of hire
- Qualifying life events allow mid-year changes
- Benefits information available on company intranet
- HR benefits specialist available for questions and support
